Can't Find That Special Thing You Want? Well, here is a list of friends (and link to their sites) that are also in the pastry business!
<Le Cakery>
Le Cakery has been around for a while, and we remember going there as children. The chefs are extremly talented and they make great pastries.
<Doaba Sweets & Restaurant>
This place has been around for a long while, and we also remember going there as children. This place specializes in Indian sweets and cuisine. But The food is just too good not to recommend.
<The Cake Shop>
The Cake Shop specializes in cake, and are very good at baking and icing them. If you want a cake that is not made by us, then go to Le Cakery; And if you don't like their cakes, then go to The Cake Shop.
<Life of Pie>
As the name suggests, this shop specializes in pie. If you really love pie, then this is the place for you!
