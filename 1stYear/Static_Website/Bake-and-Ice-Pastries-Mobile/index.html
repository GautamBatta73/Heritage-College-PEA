Bake and Ice Pastries bake the greatest pasties known to mankind. Based in Ottawa, it is family and friend owned and operated. It offers The World's greatest sweet treats for any Occasion.
We offer the largest variety of delicious cakes, donuts, cookies, and more!
Our baked goods are world-renowned, and have been enjoyed by celebrities from all over this planet.

Our amazing staff is committed to our mission of access to high quality pastries for all. We offer fantastic hours and low prices, so you can get that amazing treat you love. Our satisfied customers have a lot to say. If you would like to provide feedback about our services, please fill out our Feedback Form. We also have many other pastry shops as friends, in case you disliked our services.
